Our Story

Since 1985, Sprinkles has been one ofthe most-cherished bakers and set the standard for desserts and baked goods. It is a journey from being the neighborhood gem to sweet success. And while we have grown, a few things will always remain: open early and close late, a dollop of buttercream makes everything better, and our mission: make today sweeter than yesterday.

Join Our Team

Hospitality is at the heart of the Sprinkles experience. We believe a respectful, empathetic working environment is crucial to creating a positive team experience and keeping our guests happy. Interested in one of the sweetest gigs around? Do contact us.

Giving Back

Even though we’re famous worldwide, Magnolia Bakery remains a neighborhood bakery at heart. We’re committed to serving our local communities by supporting small businesses, charities, schools, and bake sales. If you’re seeking a product donation for your cause, reach out to sprinkles@cake.com to tell us more!
